About Me

My name's Kritika Saran. I am 21 years old. I am pursuing the Integrated Programme in Management at IIM Rohtak. I am very keen to learn new skils. I like to paint, dance, cook food, propagate my plants, knit and style clothes. I am planning to learn how to stitch this year.

My hobby is playing with makeup and trying new styles that suit me. I am thinking to pursue the art of face painting.

I am also pursuing an HR internship in an IT startup company. I am a part of an organization named Prshni where I head the social media marketing platforms. I wish to become an entrepreneur of a trendy,ethical clothing and makeup line.

The quarantine period made me introduce to new realms of life. I have stepped towards living a sustainable lifestyle. I have left meat and planning to leave dairy products. I have started meditating which helps me keep my calm. Meditation has helped me to become more mindful of my choices. I realised how we compare monetary aspect to the satisfaction of our lives.

I wish to become successful, and thrive to grow more at same time to be content with my success.
