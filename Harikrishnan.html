About Me

My name is R Harikrishnan. I come from Kollam, Kerala. I am 19 years old; I was born on January 18th, 2002. I'm currently a third-year student in the integrated programme in management course from the Indian Institute of Management Rohtak. I live with my father and mother. My father is a Chief Engineer in Maersk Tankers, and my mother is a homemaker. Curious, Determined, and Optimistic are three words that I believe describes me. I consider my friends as my most prized possession because I can open up to them at any time, and they make me a better person. I am currently doing an internship at Reach Technologies as a member of the growth hacking team. The Portuguese footballer Cristiano Ronaldo is my biggest inspiration; his rise from poverty and overcoming racing heart disorder to become a professional footballer and then being diagnosed with tendinosis in 2014 and still going on to win 3 more Ballon d'Or is something out of a fairy tale. Now even at the age of 36, he is outperforming youngsters at both club and international levels.
